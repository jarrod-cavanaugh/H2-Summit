Second contact on VERSARE and Dabur
Both are single-threaded. Dole doesn't carry the same urgency given where it realistically sits, but VERSARE and Dabur are live enough that losing the one relationship on each would cost real pipeline.
Email and chat access on phone — standing issue, not just for shows
We're in sales — nights, weekends, and travel are when deals move, and not having real-time email and chat access on my phone is the tech debt most likely to cost a live deal the response time it needed, any week of the year.
CRM hygiene — no contacts on most companies, plus duplicates
HubSpot's gotten a lot better, but of the 2,375 companies in my territory, 1,612 (68%) have zero contacts attached — every time I do outreach on my own, I'm hunting down a contact from scratch instead of pulling one from the record. There are also straight duplicates sitting in the system — Chicco alone has 3 separate company records. Both cost real time on top of the outreach itself.
Commission payout clarity — visibility within 48 hours of a win
Not blocking pipeline today, but becomes real the moment Dabur or VERSARE closes — want this settled before it's live money, not after.
Why this matters at the company level: H2 needs $24.3M/month team-wide — roughly 2x H1's realized pace. Karsten and Exec-Led can't carry that alone; the plan depends on the $0 AEs producing, and producing requires pipeline that doesn't yet exist. I'm doing what's in my control — my own outbound, the reopen list above, and the trade show engine. What's not in my control is top-of-funnel volume at scale, and that's the piece that needs to move for H2 to be reachable.
